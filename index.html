About Me

My name is Bryan Castleman. I earned a BA in Art from California State University, Long Beach in 2006.

I worked as a Graphic Designer for MACTEC Engineering and Consulting while in college. After college, I did some freelance graphic design for various companies like Boardworks, R.I.P.P.E.D, and VARI - Kitesurf Equipment.

In 2010, I dabbled in web design. I built a website for AEHI, Inc., an aerospace parts supplier. I used software like Adobe Dreamweaver and Adobe Flash.

I soon became interested in learning more about HTML, CSS, and Javascript and I am currently enrolled at The Coding Bootcamp at UCLA Extension.
